⚠️Unsure where to go?⚠️

If you're unsure of your assigned role for the day, please check with a team lead, or supervisor before beginning. Each role—whether it’s Parking, Shopper/Registration, or Reindeer—has specific responsibilities that contribute to the overall success of the operation.

Clear communication and role awareness ensure tasks are completed efficiently, reduce confusion, and help us provide a smooth and positive experience for everyone involved.

Remember: we’re all part of one team, and when each person understands their role, everything runs better. Don’t hesitate to ask questions or confirm your assignment before jumping in!

General Help!

  • 🛠️ Action Guide

    Use the button in the lower right corner to access key actions based on your role—whether you're managing parking, shopping for guests, or delivering packages as a reindeer. Below is a quick guide to help you choose the right job and understand your responsibilities:

    • Jobs and their roles:

  • 🚗 Parking Management

    Parking team members are responsible for adding vehicles to the map, assigning them to available parking spots, and setting key information such as language preference (English or Spanish). They help ensure that incoming guests are visible and organized for the rest of the team.

  • 🛒 Shopper / Registration

    Shoppers manage car statuses to keep all systems up to date. By updating statuses accurately and promptly, they help ensure a smooth experience and give the team a real-time understanding of vehicle progress and estimated wait times.

  • 🎁 Reindeer (Delivery Team)

    Reindeer are in charge of removing cars from the map after presents (or bags) have been delivered. Please only remove a car once all bags have been handed off to the vehicle. If you remove a car by mistake, don’t worry—a local "Undo Remove" button is available in the bottom left corner of your screen.
