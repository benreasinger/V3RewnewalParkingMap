General Help:

🛠️ Action Guide

Use the button in the lower right corner to access key actions based on your role—whether you're managing parking, shopping for guests, or delivering packages as a reindeer. Below is a quick guide to help you choose the right job and understand your responsibilities:


Status Color Key:
1. In-Car
2. Fetch-to-shop
3. Registration Complete
4. Shopping
5. Wrapping
6. Waiting for Reindeer

Jobs and their roles:

🚗 Parking Management


For all parking lot attendants Manage every aspect of the parking process within the app. Attendants can add new vehicles, assign the correct parking spot, set the preferred language, update details when needed, or remove cars once they leave. This ensures accurate records and smooth operation across the lot.

🛒 Registration / Shopper


For Registration Assistants and Shopping Gate Keepers Oversee the registration and shopping stages by updating vehicle status as families check in and move through the shopping process. Helps track progress and keeps the flow organized from start to finish.

🎁 Wrapping Team


For Wrapping Gate Keepers and Wrapping Assistants Update vehicle status during the wrapping stage. Mark when gifts have arrived in the wrapping room and confirm again once everything is fully wrapped and ready for delivery. Ensures all packages are accounted for before leaving.

🦌 Reindeer (Delivery Team)


For Reindeer volunteers in the wrapping area Handle the final step of the process by updating the app once all wrapped gifts are delivered to the correct vehicle. This confirms completion and removes the car from the system.

Unsure where to go?

If you're unsure of your assigned role for the day, please check with a team lead, or supervisor before beginning. Each role—whether it’s Parking, Shopper/Registration, or Reindeer—has specific responsibilities that contribute to the overall success of the operation.

Clear communication and role awareness ensure tasks are completed efficiently, reduce confusion, and help us provide a smooth and positive experience for everyone involved.

Remember: we’re all part of one team, and when each person understands their role, everything runs better. Don’t hesitate to ask questions or confirm your assignment before jumping in!
